Handmade – Meaning every bath is completely unique, Fits through doors making renovating bathrooms easier, Tough – but also repairable
Eco sensitive – Utilizing reclaimed materials such as crushed quartz, marble and stone, Strongest composite stone on the market comprising of quality materials
Totally customizable sizes for all bathrooms and outdoor projects.
